Our Story

Bridging the Gap

NaCl Remote was founded on a simple belief: Talent is universal. Opportunity is not.

As demand for remote talent grows in the U.S., there is an equally strong supply of skilled, motivated professionals in Kenya. However, barriers such as access, trust, and hiring infrastructure have limited this connection.

NaCl Remote exists to bridge that gap by providing:

  • Rigorous vetting processes
  • Clear communication standards
  • Structured hiring workflows
  • Ongoing client and talent support
